Who is
Caio Terra?

Caio Terra is one of the most decorated and technical Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu practitioners in the world. Known for his revolutionary approach to the ground game, he has dominated competition floors across the globe — from local championships to the most prestigious stages in the sport.

His philosophy centers on precision over power, timing over strength, and deep understanding over brute athleticism. Caio has dedicated his life not just to competing, but to teaching — creating a lineage of highly technical practitioners across his worldwide association.

Your Hosts

Zahid Team CTA

Casablanca, Morocco

Zahid Team CTA is an exceptional Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu team consisting of devoted practitioners passionate about their craft in Casablanca. The team shares a strong sense of community and support, inspiring members to strive consistently for growth and improvement.

Led by experienced instructor Mohamed Zahid, the team trains hard and diligently to compete at high levels in both local and regional competitions. What truly sets Zahid Team apart is the camaraderie among its members — they embody the true spirit of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu as a martial art and way of life.

In 2023, Zahid Team joined the Caio Terra Association because they felt that CTA's missions and values align with their own — becoming the first Moroccan affiliate of the association.
